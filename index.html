PB&J Recipe

Learn how to make a simple Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (grape Jelly)

Instructions

  1. Lay out two slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on one side
  3. Spread jelly on the other side
  4. put two slices together
